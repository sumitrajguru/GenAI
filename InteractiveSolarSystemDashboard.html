☀️ The Sun

Type: Star
Temperature: 5,778°C surface
Fun Fact: The Sun is so big that 1.3 million Earths could fit inside it!

☿️ Mercury

Distance from Sun: 58 million km
Day Length: 59 Earth days
Fun Fact: Mercury has no atmosphere and huge temperature swings!

♀️ Venus

Distance from Sun: 108 million km
Day Length: 243 Earth days
Fun Fact: Venus is the hottest planet and spins backwards!

🌍 Earth

Distance from Sun: 150 million km
Day Length: 24 hours
Fun Fact: Earth is the only planet we know with life and liquid water!

♂️ Mars

Distance from Sun: 228 million km
Day Length: 24.6 hours
Fun Fact: Mars has the largest volcano in the solar system!

♃ Jupiter

Distance from Sun: 778 million km
Moons: 95 known moons
Fun Fact: Jupiter is so big it could fit all other planets inside it!

♄ Saturn

Distance from Sun: 1.4 billion km
Special Feature: Beautiful rings
Fun Fact: Saturn is less dense than water - it would float!

⛢ Uranus

Distance from Sun: 2.9 billion km
Rotation: Spins on its side
Fun Fact: Uranus is the coldest planet in our solar system!

♆ Neptune

Distance from Sun: 4.5 billion km
Wind Speed: Up to 2,100 km/h
Fun Fact: Neptune has the strongest winds in the solar system!
